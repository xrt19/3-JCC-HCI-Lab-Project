Events

What's on the calendar?

Engaging international conferences, exhibitions, fairs, thrilling indoor sports, and enchanting musical concerts. Countless unforgettable moments await at the venue.

Less is More: A Minimalist Art Exhibition
October 5-30, 2023
Don't miss this chance to explore the beauty and simplicity of minimalist art at our upcoming exhibition. Featuring works from a range of talented artists, including Donald Judd, Agnes Martin, and Dan Flavin, this exhibition is a celebration of the power of minimalism.

Individualizing Medical Conference by Mayo Clinic
October 20-22, 2023
Discover the Individualizing Medical Conference: where healthcare professionals and researchers unite to advance personalized medicine. Explore trends, technologies, and ethics in tailoring care to each patient. Join informative sessions, workshops, and networking for collaboration in individualized healthcare.

2024 Jakarta International Book Fair
March 15-19, 2024
The 2024 Jakarta International Book Fair is a must-attend event for book lovers from around the world. Featuring hundreds of exhibitors and thousands of books, this fair is a celebration of the written word in all its forms. Whether you're an avid reader, a writer, or a publisher, you won't want to miss this exciting event.

OECD Secretary-General and Central Bank Governors Meeting
May 15-17, 2024
The OECD Secretary-General and Central Bank Governors Meeting brings influential leaders together to shape global economic policies, foster cooperation, and address critical financial matters. This pivotal event, attended by government officials, economists, and financial experts, plays a significant role in shaping the future of international economic governance.
