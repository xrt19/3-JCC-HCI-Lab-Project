Jockey’s Conference Center
With an extensive history that goes back to its establishment in 1974, Jakarta, Indonesia's JCC (Jockey's Convention Center) has grown into a well-known conference venue. Numerous prominent national and international conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sporting occasions, and musical shows have collectively taken place there as well, including the Asian-African Conference and the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference. The JCC continuing to be highly regarded all year long as one of Jakarta's greatest and oldest convention venues.
